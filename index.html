NOAH'S CARE BAGS AT A GLANCE

To support families going through a crisis by providing necessary items to parents/guardians to care for themselves allowing them to focus on their child who is hospitalized. We provide shampoo, condition, soap, lotion, toothbrush, toothpaste, and some candy along with a positive message to parents/guardians of children admitted to Golisano Children's Hospital at Upstate Medical University.
